QuickPay

QucikPay is the easiest and qucikest way to get connected to our online payment system.

What is QuickPay?

QuickPay allows merchants to collect payments online for their products or services without needing expensive software.

For example,a customer orders an airline ticket from a travel agency over the phone.Traditionally,that customer would have to go to the travel agency to pay.

With QuickPay,the travel agency is provided with a customized online from that allows the customer to pay without leaving the house.All the customer has to do is enter his/her name,email,telephone number,order number,and credit card information.The merchant will then get notification that the payment was made.

Why QuickPay?

QuickPay is the ideal solution for most small- and medium-sized business.Here is why:

  • Fully Hosted Solution:
    No need to install any software on your own servers
  • Quick and Easy setup:
    Your company/organization will be online inone week
    (one contacts are signed)
  • Customized design:
    Your payment pages will be customised to fit your own organization
  • Minimal Effort from You:
    All you need to do is place a button on your site that links tou your QuickPay solution
  • Admin Panel:
    View transactions using our convenient admin panel

Get Connected

If you have a website and would like to collect payments online,please contact contact@goldenpay.az today!

If you are a company,we will need the following:

  • Company's Legal Name and Address
  • Bank details of the account you are going to use to collect money
  • Information about what the company will sell online

If you are an individual,we will need:

  • A copy of Tax Card
  • A copy of your national ID card
  • Bank details of the account you are going to use to collect money
    (you do not need to create an account specially for this)
    Please note that your e-Commerce bank account must be with one of our Partners Bank
  • Information about what the company will sell online
