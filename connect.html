How it works

Ever wondered how an e-Commerce transaction works? Find out here!

Process

Any client wanting to purchase a service or product online goes to merchant's website and chooses the product or service they would like to purchase.Then the client gets re-directed to the payment gateway website,which looks like exactly like the merchant's website.The client then enters his credit card information in this super-secure site.Any client wanting to purchase a service or product online goes to merchant's website and chooses the product or service they would like to purchase.Then the client gets re-directed to the payment gateway website,which looks like exactly like the merchant's website.The client then enters his credit card information in this super-secure site.

In Azerbaijan,GoldenPay uses MilliKart's Processing Center for the process descriped above.Clients can hold any Visa/MasterCard,Visa Electron/Maestro card from any bank in the world.

Security

GoldenPay takes your online security seriously.We encrypt every online online payment transaction using TLS/SSL.

Security

GoldenPay uses GeoTrust to encrypt all of its transactions. GeoTrust is a world leader in SSL(Secure Socket Layer) and TLS(TransPort Layer Security) encryption.

Availability

One of the best things about e-Commerce is that you never have to "close".No matter what time it is,people from all over the world can visit your site and do business with you.And since GoldenPay processes payments 24/7, you can collect payments/orders even when you're at home or on vocation.

Reliability

We guarantee 99.9% payment processing system uptime.Our stat-of-the-art monitoring systems constantly inform us of the system's status,so we know about any problems right away.You can sleep easy knowing that your site will always be open for business.

Global Focus

GoldenPay allows you to offer your products/services online to a global audience,instead of just focusing locally.This translates to millions of additional people being able to see what you have to offer.This is an unprecedented advantage to e-Commerce.

Increased Profit Margins

An e-Commerce solutiongives your customers greater choice in how they can do business with you.Before,a customer my choose not to come to your shop because it is too far away.Now,that same customer can go to your website and buy directly from you.

Low Overhead

When compare to opening a new physical location, an a-Commerce solution is incredible cheap.Connection to the payment gateway costs only 150 AZN(the yearly support fee is 200AZN).The only other costs are hosting and website development.In addition,it takes only one or two staff members to manage an e-Shop,whereas it might take five or ten to manage a physical store.

Fast Integration

Once contracts are signed,we'll get you connected in a matter of days.If you're doing the connection work yourself,we'll make sure the process is as smooth as possible.

Qualified Customer Support

If your customers have problems paying problems,our customer support team is avaliable 24 hours a day to help.
